Plant cell diagram:
  • Mitochondria: Known as the powerhouse of the cell, mitochondria produce energy through cellular respiration.
  • Cell Wall: Found in plant cells, the cell wall provides structural support and protection to the cell.
  • Chloroplast: Also present in plant cells, chloroplasts are responsible for photosynthesis, converting light energy into chemical energy.
  • Golgi Apparatus: This organelle processes and packages proteins and lipids synthesized by the cell.
  • Nucleus: The nucleus contains the cell's genetic material and controls cellular activities.
  • Vacuole: Large membrane-bound sacs found in plant and some animal cells, vacuoles store water, nutrients, and waste products.
